## üõ° Insurance

### Required Coverage

**General Liability Insurance**
- Property damage and bodily injury coverage
- Minimum $1M/$2M recommended

**Commercial Auto Insurance**
- For all company vehicles and equipment transport

**Workers' Compensation**
- Required if you have employees
- State-mandated coverage

**Inland Marine / Equipment Insurance**
- Covers tools, equipment, and materials

**Vacant Property Insurance**
- Special coverage for work on vacant properties
- Many standard policies exclude vacant homes

### Insurance Resources

**The Hartford - Contractor Insurance**
- https://www.thehartford.com/business-insurance/contractors

## üìù Next Steps Checklist

### Business Formation (Week 1)
- [ ] Form LLC with NC Secretary of State
- [ ] Obtain EIN from IRS
- [ ] Open business bank account
- [ ] Get business insurance quotes (GL, Auto, WC, Equipment)

### Licensing (Week 2-3)
- [ ] Apply for NC landscape contractor license (if required)
- [ ] Research general contractor license requirements
- [ ] Obtain surety bonds if needed
- [ ] Register with local municipalities

### Operations Setup (Week 2-4)
- [ ] Purchase equipment (mowers, tools, truck)
- [ ] Sign up for Jobber or Housecall Pro
- [ ] Set up QuickBooks Online
- [ ] Create service contracts and pricing
- [ ] Prepare insurance certificates (COI)

### Data & Leads (Week 3-4)
- [ ] Start ATTOM or RealtyTrac trial
- [ ] Monitor Auction.com for NC properties
- [ ] Set up alerts on HUD Homes
- [ ] Contact 3 county clerk offices for foreclosure lists

### Marketing (Week 4-6)
- [ ] Create vendor packet (overview + services + insurance + contract)
- [ ] Reach out to 10 local banks' REO departments
- [ ] Apply to Safeguard, Altisource, United Field Services
- [ ] Connect with 5 local REO agents
- [ ] Join local property management associations
